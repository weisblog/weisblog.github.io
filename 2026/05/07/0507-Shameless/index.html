Shameless

Posted on 2026-05-07 | In Reviews

一口气看完了Shameless剩下剧情。怎么说呢，从底层爬起来从来不是轻松的事情，而对Fiona的离开更多是中剧情杀，Lip更是从未成熟果的感觉，Debbie探索半天更是没有成为她想要的好母亲。Ian倒是和Mickey终成眷属，算是happy ending，Carl成为警察，liam成为着墨不多。至于Frank更是后面变得可怜起来，demential和老而无依，最后更是只有Frany和Fiona是他的羁绊。虽然剧情不咋地，但是最后Frank的演技着实大赞，尤其是演失忆和痴呆的时候。

讽刺，真的拉满啊，从头到尾，不忍再看一次。

现在也没心思大书特书，仔细分析，简单摘抄些有意思的句子吧

When options are few, the wise man adapts.

Did Mo make a mistake, yes he did. And he regrets it. And more importantly, he paid for it. He went to jail. He’s been rehabilitated. The system works.

I think what we all have to keep in mind here is that Mo’s a great congressman, and before that, he was the best football coach that Robert E Lee High ever had. And do you know what’s even more American than football? Second chances.

Hell, this whole country was built on second chances. O.J. got one. He blew it but he got one. Teddy Kennedy, the man you don’t want behind the wheel if you need a ride to the other end of the bridge? Reelected seven times. And admit it, we are all pulling for Tiger every time he walks on the course.

My point is, we all make mistakes. Don’t we all want a second chance? Man, I know I do. And for the South Side, Mo White is that second chance.
