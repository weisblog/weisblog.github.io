the Art Institute of Chicago

Posted on 2022-12-11 | In Travel , Museum

芝加哥博物馆，神往已久，来这之前就知道那几幅很出名的画作，Seurat的《大碗岛的星期天下午》，还有霍普的美国《夜鹰》等几幅美国名画。闲话少叙，吃过煎饼果子就直奔the Art Institude of Chicago!

尽管需要存包让我颇感不爽，一进大厅，面对南北朝和唐代佛像瞬间平静下来，果然民生愈疾苦，神佛愈悲悯。直接前往二楼的impressionism，没想到第一眼竟然是Gustave的《Paris Street；Rainy Day》，这份不期而至的惊喜点亮了一天，没想到这第一个印象派的街头画居然是在这里。随后更加细致的欣赏发现这个博物馆和MET和MFA相比，有更多的文艺复兴晚期作品（1480～15**），也有挺多Baroque和Rococo作品，尤其是Rubens的小幅画作，可惜没有卡拉瓦乔和贝尼尼的，rococo拍的也少；它也有完整的Dutch painting介绍（有点累没细看）；其中realism和symbolism着实让我大开眼界；之后去了三楼的现代馆，领略Picasso的风格转变、野兽派的Matisse、Dada、sur-realism的Dali和Magritte，不过也是浮光掠影罢了；临走之前去瞅了眼中国瓷器，特精没有，不过有个元青花印象深刻。鉴于今天看的主要是19 20 世纪的绘画，相比起来，立体主义和表现主义的绘画较少，Cezanne的画我更是没找到，否则堪称脉络最为清晰的一个博物馆了

下面就按时间介绍作品吧，虽说我逛的时候不是按此顺序。

Renaissance 晚期作品：神性

Sano di Pietro, 1405-1481

Matteo di Giovanni, 1430-1495

In Siena and Florence, the tradition of bringing delicacies on a tray to a mother led to the development of this new format of painted luxury object, which often featured allegorical （寓言）scenes (on the front) and images of children at play and family heraldry (文章on the back). Over time these painted deschi ceased to be functional objects and instead served as decor for the bedchamber.

Antonio Vivarini, 1415-1476

Robes of Dominican friar, this type of altarpiece of saints was rather old-fashioned in mid-15th-century Venice

Paolo Veneziano, 1333-1358, Venetian

Combined sinuous Gothic contours, sumptuous textile patterns, and severa facial types influenced by contemporary Byzantine art. 西欧和拜占庭帝国的影响交汇

Tintoretto, 1518-1584, Italian

矫饰主义。原名雅科波·康明（意大利语：Jacopo Comin）[1]，是意大利文艺复兴晚期最后一位伟大的画家，和提香、委罗内塞并称为威尼斯画派的“三杰”之一

The Holy Family. 圣母在中间：地位在天主教上升；while her pyramidal arrangement shows Rubens’s attention to the principles of Renaissance art.

Rubens conveyed the saint’s spirituality through his meditative gaze and posture.

劳特累克承袭印象派画家奥斯卡-克劳德·莫内、卡米耶·毕沙罗等人画风，以及日本浮世绘之影响，开拓出新的绘画写实技巧。他擅长人物画，对象多为巴黎蒙马特一带的舞者、女伶、妓女等中下阶层人物。

In the 188os, at a time when many of the original Impressionist painters had begun to pursue independent styles, Camille Pissarro actively worked to keep the group together.毕沙罗喜好写生，画了相当多的风景画，他的后期作品是印象派中点彩画派的佳作，此外，毕沙罗的人像画也有他特殊的风格。

Hams Thoma, 1839-1924, German

Hans Thoma was a leading Northern European figure in the late 19th-century shift from Naturalism to art inspired by classical myths and legends.

The subject of this work, taken from Ovid’s epic poem Metamorphoses, is the satyr Marsyas challenging Apollo, the master of the lyre, to a musical contest. Although the artist avoided depicting the cruel outcome of the match (the satyr lost and was flayed alive by Apollo), his treatment of Apollo, whose idealized body and luminous skin set him apart from the shadowy halftones of his challenger, hints at the winner.

Pierre Cecile Puvis de Chavannes, 1824-1898, French

Porcelain

越窑

钧窑

Jun ware is named after its original production center, located in an area in north-central China once known as Junzhou. Its distinctively cloudy yet lustrous lavender-blue or robin’s-egg-blue glaze is one of the most intriguing achievements of Chinese potters.

By prolonging the cooling of the kiln at the end of its firing cycle, potters induced the glaze to separate and bubble with minute glassy crystals, which reflect and scatter blue light like the sky on a clear day.

鲁山窑

Lushan or Huangdao ware, named after its major production sites in north-central China, is distinguished by two layers of glaze like those coating this large, exuberant jar: a brownish-black that thins to finely crazed olive brown and a cream shading to violet blue mottled with tan. The first layer, applied by dipping the vessel upside down, stops unevenly above the foot; the second, splashed or poured over overlapping areas of the shoulder, cascades down the body in diagonal streaks, as if pulled by the momentum of a rotating wheel.

胎质较粗，胎色浅灰褐；釉色有黑釉、白釉和三彩釉等。

百年后石破天惊的汝窑就是在柴窑的基础上创烧的，而清凉寺离这里也不远。这就是后人称为的柴窑，应该就在鲁山一带。

耀州窑

耀州窯是唐朝至明朝時的青瓷窯之一，位於陕西省铜川市黃堡鎮，始於唐代，到宋代時最為興盛，為六大窯系中最大的，是北方青瓷的代表。

耀州窯主要的裝飾方法，主要有刻花、剔花、印花、鏤空等。

建窑

主要产品以盏、碗、盘、碟为主，兼烧盒、罐、壶、灯、炉、钵、梅瓶、冥器等，在宋代盛产黑釉瓷而闻名于世。黑釉盏的特征：胎含铁量高，一向有“铁胎”之称，胎体厚重，呈黑灰色、紫黑色，胎质粗糙坚硬，露胎处色沉而无光。

青白窑

青白瓷也叫“影青”、“隐青”、“映青”。指的是釉色介于青白二色之间，青中泛白、白中透青的一种瓷器。青白瓷是宋元时期景德镇及受其影响的窑场烧成的、具有独特风格和鲜明时代特征的新品种。

元青花

The early-14th-century introduction of cobalt-blue ore, probably from Persia (modern-day Iran), was a true watershed in the development of Chinese porcelain.In 1279, China had capitulated to the rule of Kublai Khan, who founded China’s Yuan dynasty within his vast Mongol empire.From the I4th century on, all major developments in Chinese porcelain would originate in Jingdezhen.

With the comingling of Chinese and Mongol craftsmen came a hybrid of ceramic techniques and styles. Blue and white, the most important among these, developed
