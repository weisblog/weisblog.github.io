The last duel

Posted on 2022-01-02 | In Reviews

罗生门似的故事，很精彩，从不同角度不同倾向地叙述了中世纪婚姻、夫妻、性关系和名誉。无论是一开始两个人对战争中形象的讲述，还是后来关键的城堡affair，入木三分、引人入胜，谁是谁非却又怎么说得清楚，还有一些锁门、脱鞋的细节刻画，遑论眼神、喘气、噤声、挣扎、哭泣，很不错。基本上可以确定的是Jean娶妻完全是为了heir，毫无情趣和感情。他为人甚至粗鲁，但没人能否认他是个勇敢无畏的knight，用生命捍卫尊严和名誉。十四世纪，查理六世（疯子查理演得也不错），emmm应该是和金雀花王朝正在进行百年战争吧。还有Marguerite被强暴后的心神不宁，知道好友pregnant后复杂的心绪和遭遇背叛时的痛苦绝望都被展现的淋漓尽致，一句“Oh I missed you more than you know”包含了多少无助依恋委屈和期盼，第一次出现的时候我只是稍感突兀，觉得这个恭维有点不自然，后来才知道满满的都是戏啊。乖乖。最后说一下，审判时怀孕简直点睛之笔，孩子到底是谁的？关于怀孕的莫名其妙的医学推断，Did you experience pleasure at the end of intercourse? 据此来判断到底是affair还是rape，这就是中世纪吗？


摘抄点句子吧

Rule No. 24: A new love expels an old one.

Rule No. 4: Love is always growing or diminishing.

Rule No. : Nothing prevents a woman from being loved by two men. Or a man from being loved by two women.


We forgive a child afraid of the dark. Tragedy is men afraid of the light.


Appreciation of one another’s extraordinary good looks.


To even think is to covet, my lord. – No less sinful than her coveting me.


The debt we owe to our friend is the greatest of all.

– Is it greater than love?


Formally, this is not about her. Rape is not a crime against a woman. It is a property crime against her male guardian.


A good soldier and good fortune are not long parted.

– I returned a knight. That is the second time you do not call me “Sir”. There will not be a third.

– No offense intended, Sir Jean.

– I will not be patronized by this squire who lies about court waiting to be feted with gift upon gift upon gift and risks nothing. Nothing.


Milady, forgive me for intruding. I love you more than any other, and I would do anything for you. Everything I have is yours.

– How can you speak to me like this?

– Marguerite, what way then would I speak to you? Milady you must know it can not be helped. My love for you has consumed me.

– How dare you.

I am married. – Yes. You are saddled with a terrible burden. A cold, callous man who does not love you as I do, who can not appreciate you for the marvel that you are.

– Please. (M: do not speak of my husband)– I only want for your happiness. I can not bear to see you in such hardship. I know your house’s finances are fragile.

– My lord provides me. – Yes… But does he know you to be the most exquisite,… fascinating woman? …A women … I would devote my life to. Here me say I am in love with you. I know you love me, too. You must.

…

You fear yourself guilty, but, my love, I beseech you, tell no one. For your own safety. If your husband hears of this, he may kill you. Say nothing. I will keep quite too. Do not fell bad, my love. We could not help ourselves.


Jean and Marguerite’s father:

My daughter comes with the money and the land that I’ve offered. That is all I own. And what of the bride price?

– I offer you a name of value. I should expect she’s capable of performing her wifely duties? Bearing an heir?

– My daughter is strong and healthy and ready to honor the marriage. The conception of an heir and children to follow shall be shift. And prolific .


Marguerite 和 Jean妈妈

– You are no different from the peasants our soldiers have their way with when they are at war. Do they complain?

– I can not be silent. I must speak.

– Marguerite, all you have done is bring shame to our family.

– I am telling the truth.

– The truth does not matter. You look at me as if were never young. I was raped. And despite my protestations and my revulsion, did I go crying to my lord, who had better things to worry about? No, I stood up, got on with life

– But at what cost?

– At what cost? I am alive.

– Well, you have paid a dear price for that privilege.


Jean and Marguerite

I begged you to find another way, and now I may be burned alive.

– I am risking my life for you.

– You are risking my life so you can fight your enemy and save your pride. And that could render our child an orphan. Or did you not think of that? You are a hypocrite. You are blinded by your vanity.


If I had known the truth would deprive me of this love, I believe I would have done what many women before me had done. Nothing.

–What you did was right.

– Nor if I die. I am not like you Jean.
