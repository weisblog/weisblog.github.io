爱死机

Posted on 2022-06-01 | In Reviews

爱死机一如既往得大开眼界，除开第一集为了衔接而稍显窠臼，都是极好的。

Episode 1: Three robots: Exit Strategies. A doom fantasy and people’s results from different strategies, full of political correct and sarcasm. To warn the world with pessimistic ending.

Episode 2: Bad Traveling. Based on the Age of Discovery, having a strong British accent, inspired by Faust dealing with Mephistopheles. A schemer has a deal with a evil creature(crab) and seize the power of captain. Several points I think unpractical, you don’t usually have women on ship, a symbol of a jinx. Also, captain has the absolute power on vessel, no democracy there. I like the several plot twists and the frame, especially killed the strongest men with a simple deception. And all crosses really make sence.

Episode 3: The very pulse of the mathine A dead astronaut becomes a media, trans-passing thoughts between astronaut and IO, which is a machine. The big eye of Jupiter, the volcanoes on IO, all nice to see. And remember, don’t go to places you ought not to.

Episode 4: 丧尸导致的世界毁灭

Episode 5: 关于无敌战争机器基因改造钢铁熊失去控制的故事。

Episode 6: Swarm. 我只能说牛逼的想象, 语言苍白，虫族万岁！

Episode 7: Mason’s rat. 西部大农场的鼠患，先进科技和不屈意志的对抗，科技文明和自然生命的讨论，着实偏左。

Episode 8: 有点扯淡。

Episode 9: Jibaro。 无声和有声的转换（一个聋子士兵的视角），中世纪武士和巫女，勇气和怯懦，贪婪和欲望，美色和金钱。暗讽印第安或者南美殖民的感觉。舞蹈有拉丁和桑巴的影子，但背景和音乐又颇有中国禅宗的那种影子。
