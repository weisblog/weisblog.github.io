艺术历史博物馆

Posted on 2025-05-01 | In Travel , Europe , Vienna , Museum

博物馆还是要看一看的，法国意大利一般般吧，倒是德奥的还不错，巴洛克风格的天顶画也是极好的。

Michelangelo Merisi, gen. CaravaggiO (1571-1610),

Crowning of Thorns, around 1603

After interrogation by Pontius Pilate, Christ is made to wear a crown of thorns by the soldiers. He is thus mocked as King of the Jews. While previously regarded as a Roman or Neapoli-tan variation of a lost Caravaggio, this picture has now been confirmed as an original by the discovery of certain docu-ments. Other arguments for its authenticity previously put forward included technical idiosyncrasies of the painter, such as the contour lines engraved with the stem of the brush to be found on Christ’s head, and on the shoulders, chest and hands

贝尼尼

虽然**詹洛伦佐·贝尼尼（Gian Lorenzo Bernini）**以雕塑和建筑最为著名，但他在早年和艺术生涯中也创作过一部分**绘画作品**，不过数量远远少于他的雕塑，且大多是私人练习、肖像画或为家族成员所作。

自然光

乔尔乔内

朦胧氛围、色彩晕染、象征隐喻、风景融入情感、视觉诗意

Paolo Veronese (1528-1588) tätig in Venedig
  • 人物众多、动作复杂、服饰华丽、场景戏剧化——这是Veronese（或其追随者）极为典型的风格，与提香和丁托列托并列为威尼斯画派三巨头。

Sacrificial Death of Marcus Curtius around 1550/52

With its bold perspective, Veronese aimed with this picture at surprising the viewer, an effect which precisely corresponded with the Mannerist taste of the period with its delight in the visually unusual and the artistically playful. The scene depict-ed here is from the mythical history of Rome: to reconcile the Gods and to save Rome, the heroic Roman Marcus Curtius, af-ter hearing the Oracle pronounce, throws himself down a fis-sure in the earth at the Forum Romanum. Perhaps originally used as ceiling decoration

Judith with the Head of Holofernes around 1582

In the hands of Veronese even this gloomy scene gains in so-lemnity by - initially - focusing on stark colours to emphasise the heroine’s worldly beauty. Only on closer inspection do we catch sight of Holofernes’ dark head and the sack that is ready to contain it, held by the servant. It is exactly this contrast be-tween light and dark, between beauty and terror, which gives the picture its Mannerist charm.

The Raising of the Youth of Nain, around 1565/70

The shimmering jewel-like tones of the robes glow before the cool background of the sky and architecture, lending a world-ly, festive effect to the New Testament scene. The element of surprise, done in typical Mannerist fashion, arises from Veronese moving the main character, the youth awakened from death, into the lower left-hand corner of the painting, only half of him being visible. The grateful mother, however, is placed in the middle of the composition.

丁托列托 Tintoretto

强烈的明暗对比（Chiaroscuro）：受卡拉瓦乔先导影响，对比强烈的光影增强戏剧张力。

快速笔触和剧烈运动感：他的画风不如提香细腻，但充满力量与张力。

庞大的宗教场景和历史画：特别擅长将复杂故事安排在层层推进的构图中。

Tizian 提香

King Acrisius of Argos locked his daughter Danaë in a tower after an oracle told him she would bear a son who would someday kill him. But Jupiter fell in love with Danaë and came down in the form of golden rain to impregnate her. Their son, Perseus, would later kill his grandfather accidentally with a discus. There are two earlier versions of this painting in Naples and Madrid, respectively, with varying additional figures.

Madonna with Child and Saints, around 1520

The dense composition of half-length figures is typical for Tit-ian’s narrative style in the 1520’s. Similar to the compositional format of an “Adoration of the Kings”, Mary with the child kicking on her lap is moved from the centre to the left to make room for the jostling saints, the ancient father of the church Hieronymus, Stephen and Maurice. A second version of the picture exists which today can be found in the Museé du Louvre, Paris.

Raphel

The Madonna of the Meadow, 1505 or 1506

The group formed into a pyramid is embedded in a delightful pasture landscape which on the one hand emphasises the world of humans; yet in the way it fades out into the far-off horizon, it appears to accentuate what is eternally valid. Thus The Madonna in the Meadow became the “icon” of the new spiritual thinking of the Renaissance.

St. Margaret, around 1518

In his last creative years, Raphael had so completely assimilated ancient art both in its essence and form that he was able effortlessly to transfer it to Christian themes, and so lend it new impact. Like a Roman Victoria, the saint triumphs over the monster. Attempts have been made to explain the rather coarse execution of this picture based on strong bright-dark contrasts as the work of assistants, yet Raphael himself may have offered the choice of a more expensive or a cheaper version of composition.

特征：丰满人体、神话宗教题材、色彩丰富、构图动感。

维米尔 Johannes Vermeer
Durer 丢勒

阿尔布雷希特·丢勒（Albrecht Dürer，1471–1528）是德国文艺复兴时期最重要的艺术家之一，也是欧洲版画史上的里程碑人物。他兼具画家、版画家、数学家与理论家身份，是将意大利文艺复兴理念引入北欧的关键人物。

他曾两度前往意大利（尤其威尼斯），接触拉斐尔、贝里尼等艺术家，吸收透视法、解剖学与古典比例。

同时保留北方画派细腻的写实传统，形成既科学又精神化的视觉风格。

Bruegel 伯努盖儿

彼得·勃鲁盖尔（Pieter Bruegel the Elder，约1525–1569）是16世纪尼德兰（今比利时和荷兰）最杰出的画家之一，被誉为“农民画家”。他以描绘农民生活、宗教寓言和社会讽刺题材著称，是北方文艺复兴晚期的代表人物之一。

勃鲁盖尔擅长描绘大规模的群体活动，如节日庆典和市场场景，画面构图复杂，充满动感。

Else

阿尔布雷希特·阿尔特多费（Albrecht Altdorfer）

画面描绘的是一个宗教题材，极可能是《基督入殓》（The Entombment of Christ），也可理解为《哀悼基督》或《安葬基督》场景

风景主导与精神氛围
背景的山景与树木非常突出，是阿尔特多费及“多瑙画派”的重要特征：风景不仅是背景，更是一种精神空间。

远山雪峰与蓝天形成神圣意境，强化宗教场景的超验性质。

  1. 人物比例与布局
    人物身形略显细长，脸部个性化但不夸张，动作自然，符合北方文艺复兴的现实主义传统。

与矫饰主义相比，没有夸张的比例扭曲或不稳定空间，场面结构稳固。

  1. 色彩与服饰
    色彩饱和：红、绿、蓝对比鲜明，服饰华丽但真实，突出北方画派对细节与质感的关注。

与意大利矫饰主义色彩冷调、光影夸张不同，这种色彩更具温度与叙事性。
